About Me

Woof! My name as you guessed is Poonsh. I am a relatively small dog, but I am very brave. For 4 years I lived on the streets. It was a very difficult and hungry time. 2 years ago, on May 24th, my parents took me from a shelter. Life is so much better with a family 💖 ! I am very important to them and I keep them fit although i am a bit lazy myself. During the pandemic i was making them to walk me so that they could get a little bit of sunlight. I have been on the streets since the childhood, these days I prefer to stay inside, but I care about my parents' health.

On this website you can learn more about my story and how much I changed since I have home. You can also visit a page with things that i like. You can pet me at any time you are on this website. I would very much appreciate it.
