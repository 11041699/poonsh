My Story's Timeline

Homeless years Prior to 2020

I spent my first 4 years in an industrial area near the railroads. I was frightened of large dogs and needed to hide from them under vehicles. No one was caring for me until...

Kind Woman Early 2020

...Until I met a kind woman who started taking care of me. She didnt bring me home but she was giving me food and made my life a lot easier. She also made me a nice haircut. After that she contacted volunteers to send me to a shelter.

Shelter time March-May 2020

At First, I spent sometime getting vaccines in a local shelter, and afterwards I travelled for a long time in a car until I reached a new big city. I was missing the woman who was helping me. It made me sad but I didnt show it to anyone. I remained calm and others started calling Buddha. However, a new woman who was taking care started calling me Poonsh. I didn't know what it meant but I liked it.

Meeting Parents May 2020 - present

In a shelter I met a lot of people who thought about adopting a pet. On May 23rd, a man and a woman came to visit. I liked them and showed them my sympathy by following them. That day they left, but the next day they came back and took me with them. This is how I found my parents.
